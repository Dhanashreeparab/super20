About us

What super20 means?

Super20 is scholarship program with batch of 20 students.In which Super stands for Skills Upgrade Program for Earning & Respect.Its 6 months free Training on soft skills and software to 20 candidates. we will also providing mentoring session with our Experts.

Our Vision

Our vision is to empower students to build there career in the field of technology. our 6 months program is divided as 3months : Shadowing a good developer and recoding somes codes. 3months : On the job development of product feature with stipend.

Join us

To join us you have to register 1st.You have to fill the correct detail for the contact. The selection process is base on aptitude test. The test will be conducted online.The required things will be infromed to you.
